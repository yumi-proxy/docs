---
title: "1. How to choose the appropriate product package?"
---

- YumiProxy offers Residential Proxy, Unlimited Residential Proxy (Bandwidth), Unlimited Residential Proxy (Port), and Static Residential Proxy – multiple flexible solutions for your choice. You can purchase based on your budget and actual needs.
- For high‑concurrency businesses, the Unlimited Residential Proxy is recommended; for cross‑border e‑commerce, the Static Residential Proxy is recommended. If your requirements are not clear, you may purchase the Basic Edition of Residential Proxy, which offers higher cost‑effectiveness.
